COP 26: A Five Point Plan for Solidarity, Prosperity and Fairness


The COP26 summit in Glasgow comes at a time of maximum need as well as maximum opportunity to deliver on the Paris Agreement’s promise of a fair, equitable and robust response to climate change. The pandemic has devastated developing countries, on top of continuing climate change impacts such as extreme heat, droughts, floods and tropical storms. Support in the form of vaccines and debt relief would send a signal that the countries least responsible for causing climate change are not alone in fighting it..
